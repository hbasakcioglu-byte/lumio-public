Privacy Policy — Lumio

Last Updated: March 29, 2026  ·  Developer: Hakan Başakçıoğlu

Lumio values your privacy. While most data is stored locally, we use industry-standard third-party services for advertising and app analytics to improve your experience.

1. Overview

Lumio is a note-taking app designed to help users capture and manage text using on-device OCR. This policy describes how we handle data through our app and third-party integrations.

2. Data Collection & Local Storage

Your notes never leave your device. All note content, book records, and saving types are stored in a local SQLite database. We do not have access to your private notes.

4. Advertising and Analytics

We use AdMob and Unity Ads to show advertisements. These providers may use your device's Advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can opt-out of personalized ads via your Android device settings.

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app (e.g., which buttons are clicked). This data is anonymized and used solely for improving the app's functionality.

5. Permissions

  • Camera: Used only for OCR. Images are processed locally and not stored or shared.
  • Internet: Required for ad delivery, analytics, and subscription verification.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage in your device's settings.
