Privacy Policy — Lumio

Effective date: March 2, 2026  ·  Developer: Hakan Başakçıoğlu

Lumio stores all your data locally on your device. We don't collect it, we don't see it, and we never will.

1. Overview

Lumio is an offline note-taking app for capturing text from books and physical sources using your camera and on-device OCR. Privacy is a core principle: your notes never leave your device.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

All content you create — notes, OCR results, book records, and saving types — is stored exclusively in a local database on your device (SQLite). This data is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

3. Camera Permission

Lumio requests camera access solely to capture images for text recognition (OCR). Images and camera frames are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded, stored externally, or shared.

4. Text Recognition (OCR)

Lumio uses Google ML Kit Text Recognition, which operates fully on-device. No image or text data is sent to Google's servers as part of the OCR process.

5. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Lumio offers an optional monthly subscription (Lumio Premium) through Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled exclusively by Google. Lumio does not collect, store, or have access to your payment information or billing details.

For details on how Google handles your payment data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Export & Import

Lumio allows you to export your notes as a JSON file to your device's local storage and import them back. This functionality is entirely local — exported files are never transmitted by Lumio.

8. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with any third party, because we do not collect any user data in the first place.

9. Data Deletion

All data exists only on your device. You can delete it at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app — removes all locally stored notes and settings.
  • Going to Settings → Apps → Lumio → Storage → Clear Data on your Android device.

10. Children's Privacy

Lumio does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. As the app collects no personal data from any user, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated effective date at the top of this page.
